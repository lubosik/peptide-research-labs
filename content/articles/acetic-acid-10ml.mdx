## Chemical Background

The compound is also known by the following synonyms: Acetic Acid, Ethanoic acid, Vinegar acid. The structural properties of Acetic Acid contribute to its function as a weak acid for laboratory research applications.

Acetic Acid is a carboxylic acid with the structure CH3COOH, consisting of a methyl group attached to a carboxyl group. The compound is a weak acid, meaning it partially dissociates in aqueous solution to form acetate ions (CH3COO-) and hydrogen ions (H+).
